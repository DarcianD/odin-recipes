Pho Recipe

Descriptions

Summer roll or "bánh tráng cuốn", is a traditional Vietnamese dish. contrary to its name, the dish is eaten year around and a very popular appetizer at Vietnamese restaurant. Its primarily consists of pork,shrimp,vegetables, and vermicelli noodles. It's wrap up in rice paper and served with peanut or fish dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pork belly
  • 1 lb of raw shrimp
  • 10 Vietnamese rice paper
  • 1 lb of vermicelli noodle
  • 2 Persian cucumbers
  • 1 head of Romaine

Instructions

  1. Boil pork and shrimp until cooked
  2. Boil vermicelli noodle until cooked
  3. Cut shrimp in half and slice pork into thin pieces
  4. Cut up cucumber and lettuce into long strip
  5. Dip rice paper into water and put on plate
  6. Places pork, shrimp, noodle, and vegetables on rice paper
  7. Wrap and serve
