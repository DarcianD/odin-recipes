Pho Recipe

Descriptions

Summer roll or "bánh tráng cuốn", is a traditional Vietnamese dish. contrary to its name, the dish is eaten year around and a very popular appetizer at Vietnamese restaurant. Its primarily consists of pork,shrimp,vegetables, and vermicelli noodles. It's wrap up in rice paper and served with peanut or fish dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pork belly
  • 1 lb of raw shrimp
  • 10 Vietnamese rice paper
  • 1 lb of rice noodle
  • 2 Persian cucumbers
  • 1 head of Romaine
